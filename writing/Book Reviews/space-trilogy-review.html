The Space Trilogy

by C.S. Lewis
Published: 1938-1945
Genre: Science Fiction
★★☆☆☆☆☆☆☆☆ 2.6/10

I didn't actually even finish this one. Read the first two books and decided it wasn't for me. These books felt like an allegory for Christianity which is cool if you know you're going into that. I was looking for alien battles and spaceships, not an exploration of Christian values through sci-fi. I can see why it's stuck around for so long but I also didn't like the writing style and found the story sort of boring/uneventful. It felt like it was written for a younger audience. Maybe it's just old? Dunno.
